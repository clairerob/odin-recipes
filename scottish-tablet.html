Honey Lavender Cold Brew Latte

Description:

A delicate and floral iced coffee drink. Homemade honey lavender syrup is a simple but luxurious accent to Oddly Correct's smooth Muffler cold brew blend.

Delicious with any milk of your choice - I recommend oat.

Ingredients:

  • 1oz honey lavender syrup:
    • 1 cup wildflower honey
    • 1 cup water
    • 3 tablespoons dried lavender
    • pinch of salt
  • 4oz Muffler cold brew concentrate
  • 4oz oat milk (or other milk)
  • ice

Steps:

  1. Make the honey lavender syrup by stirring the honey and water in a saucepan over a medium heat until fully combined.
  2. Add the lavender and salt over heat for five minutes, then let stand to cool for one hour before passing through a sieve or cheesecloth to remove the buds.
  3. In a glass, mix 1oz of honey lavender syrup with 4oz cold brew concentrate over a scoop of ice.
  4. Fill glass with ice, then top with oat milk.
  5. Optional garnish with lavender buds, and drink!
