Scottish Tablet

Description:

A traditional Scottish sweet treat, perfect to nibble alongside a freshly-brewed coffee!

Not quite a caramel, toffee, or a fudge, Scottish tablet is rich and sweet - and the homemade stuff is far and away the best you'll ever have.

Ingredients:

  • 900g/2lb granulated white sugar
  • 250ml/1 cup full-fat milk
  • 1 tin (397g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 85g/6tbsp butter

Steps:

  1. Slowly dissolve sugar, milk and butter over heat until smooth.
  2. Add condensed milk and bring to a boil, stirring continuously to avoid catching.
  3. Simmer for ~20 minutes or until the mixture darkens to a caramel color, continuing to stir.
  4. Remove from heat, beat the mixture and pour into a buttered swiss roll tin.
  5. Leave to cool before slicing into portions.
