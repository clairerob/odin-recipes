Cold Brew

Description:

The classic summer refresher with an energy boost, courtesy of Oddly Correct's smooth Muffler cold brew blend.

Ingredients:

  • 4oz Muffler cold brew concentrate
  • 4oz water
  • ice

Steps:

  1. Combine equal parts cold brew concentrate and water in a glass.
  2. Stir and fill with ice.
  3. Add sugar or creamer as desired, but this classic is great as is.
